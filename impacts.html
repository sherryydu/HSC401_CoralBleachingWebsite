What does coral bleaching impact?

Coral reefs provide so many benefits to us and other wildlife. But if bleaching continues,
we may lose our favourite animals forever.
Hover over the images to learn more.

Sea turtles

What's got four flippers and a shell? Sea turtles! Reefs are home to many of their favourite delicacies like sponges, crabs, or lobsters! As you can imagine, coral reefs are their grubbing hotspot! But if corals die, then their food dies too. Eventually, one of our favourite flippered animals may also struggle to survive.

Reef fish

Did you know that Nemo's neighbourhood was a coral reef? Clownfish are one of the many types of fish that live in reefs. When these vibrant environments lose their colour, it's like an eviction notice to these fish. Because bleaching happens so fast, many reef fish may struggle to search for a new home in time.

Humans

Coral reefs help us a lot. They increase tourism, give us seafood, and stop waves from hitting our coasts. But as corals bleach, some people may struggle to protect their homes, feed their families, and much more.
